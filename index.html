Why choose Potential?

Our mission is to champion the value of future-ready education as the heart of social responsibility.

The future must stay bright

Everywhere around the world, the quality of public education is being plagued by serious human failings, disproportionately impacting the most vulnerable. Underfunding, discrimination, censorship, student safety, overcrowding, high drop-out rates and unequal access to education have become the norm in too many countries.

Here in Ontario, the conditions created by the pandemic and the recent series of public sector cuts are amplifying the issues students have been facing, leaving them to face the future less prepared than ever before.

We build socially-responsible educational solutions that genuinely prepare students for the future they will build.

Professionally designed

Our strongest opportunities for learning dont happen when we are simply fed information or given instruction. They happen when were able to work together, question, listen and discover.

Great educators understand this and know how to best enable cultures for learning. The Potential Education Company is built by such certified education professionals from Ontario, Canada.

We build scaled and singular educational solutions where curiosity and trust are at the heart of all our initiatives.

Mindset comes first

Learning to learn is a complex and ongoing process that includes learning to think, to analyze and to organize. It also includes learning to fail and to embrace difficulty.

Developing a learning mindset and essential soft skills cannot be an extra activity that is completed if time permitsit must lay at the foundation of how teachers teach and how students learn.

We place a cardinal importance on the development of a learning mindset, giving it parallel importance to academic success.

Reality-conscious

Equitable access to education is an issue that affects millions of students and their families, and private educational services are not usually known to be cost effective.

We design our services with the reality of budget-consciousness in mind, with simple terms of service. Our small-group sessions allow families to separate the cost of sessions without negatively impacting the quality of education.

We help families and teachers find educational support solutions for students in need. Our priority will always be to best position students to learn.

Support public education

No tutoring service can ever replace school teachers&nbsp they are of unrivalled importance when it comes to a students education. We know firsthand, however, that public school classrooms cant fully meet students needs; too much gets in the way. All the more, funding cuts and the culture of deprofessionalization of educators has had profound effects on the quality of public education.

We directly support public education teachers and school programs via partnerships, program and supply grants, and curriculum-aligned resources.

Ready for (re-)imagination

Public education too often enters a state of regression because of its self-prescribed need to innovate, using uninspiring initiatives derived from past objectives that do nothing to address the future of our learners.

Revolutions in public education, large or small, happen when it allows itself to transform; when memorization is replaced with understanding and application. When conformity and compliance are replaced with curiosity, creativity and imagination.

We design all our programs and services to be future-focused. Our objective is to give all learners the knowledge and skills to be ready for what is to come.
